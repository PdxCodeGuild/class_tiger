Waffle Factory, yumm....

We are a team of waffle enthusiasts. We feel that waffles should be just as, if not more abundant than mini tacos in the gentrified community of today. The Waffle Factory brings you a quick and convenient way to pick up a tasty waffle in the morning or throughout the rest of the day. Our waffles are gluten free with the option of vegan or dairy free, to ensure your hipster dietary needs are met.

About Deez Waffles

Created with love, a boat load of butter (dairy-free available), and a pinch of angel tears. If you buy waffles from us, you will not be dissapointed.

Basic

This most simple waffle is a great starting place for your own design. Layer it up with toppings as you see fit, or keep it plain and enjoy a vegan gluten free breakfast to ease your tummy as well as your conscience.

Savory

Our Savory waffles are packed with bacon, cheese and apples with your choice of maple syrup or not. Ok, so this is more of a savory and sweet creation, but who cares just get it, you'll love it.

Sweet

The Sweet waffle option is exactly what it sounds like. Smothered with maple syrup, and your choice of traditional toppings ranging from chocalate chips to fresh fruit, this dessert waffle is sure to meet expectations.
