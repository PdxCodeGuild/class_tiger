SE Works Mission

SE Work'smission is to strengthen the economic health & well-being of our diverse community by facilitating successful connections between job seekers & employers. We have a full range of services available to job seekers, employers & youth designed to strengthen our local and regional economy. SWAPP is SE Works Youth programs Application for intake, data entry and reporting.
