Laurent Ruquier

Laurent Hugues Emmanuel Ruquier (French pronunciation: ​[loʁɑ̃ yg emanɥɛl ʁykje]; born 24 February 1963) is a French television presenter, radio host and comedian. He is also a lyricist, writer, columnist and impresario; he has been co-owner and general manager of Théâtre Antoine-Simone Berriau in Paris since 2011.
